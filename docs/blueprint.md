# **App Name**: Wealth Navigator

## Core Features:

- Dashboard: Provide an overview of financial status, including net worth, asset allocation, liabilities, and cash flow, updated in real time. the dashboard shall show me the upcoming 10 installments I will pay and upcoming 10 rents I will receive, both shall be interactive with checkboxes so when I tick any of them, they disappear and update my database accordingly.
- Asset Tracking: Tab for viewing and managing real estate assets, including location (Egypt, Turkey), rental income, and market values. User enters market values for net worth calculation.
- Liability Tracking: Tab to track liabilities, including installments for real estate projects and loans. Include payment plans and due dates. Note that data entry occurs via manual input, not document import. I want to be able to upload the supporting contracts and payment plans so the app extracts the details.
- Cash Flow Management: Overview of monthly income (salary, rents) and expenses (household in Egypt & Kuwait) with net cash flow calculation. net cash flow shall consider the monthly average deduction (by summarizing all loans and projects installments and dividing by 12)
- AI Financial Advisor: Suggest optimal rent increases based on various parameters. Generative AI tool provides personalized financial insights and recommendations based on user data to improve cash flow and investment strategies.
- Calculation Engine: Dedicated area for all calculation needs. Handles complex financial calculations related to investments, property valuations, and cash flow analysis, and supports calculations across multiple currencies.
- Report Generation: Export detailed reports, with visualizations, in PDF format for comprehensive financial analysis and sharing. excel file database shall also be exportable.
- Multi-Currency Support: the app shall be able to show money in three different currencies (EGP, USD and KWD) using the latest exchange rates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep turquoise (#46B1A5) to convey trust and sophistication, referencing the Nile's precious ecosystem and reflecting long-term wealth creation.
- Background color: Light cyan (#E0F4F1) for a calm, uncluttered user interface.
- Accent color: Coral (#FF7F50) for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and important alerts.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, will be used.
- Use a consistent set of minimalist icons throughout the app to represent various financial metrics and actions.
- Dashboard-centric design with clear sections for assets, liabilities, and cash flow. Use charts and graphs for data visualization.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user interactions and enhance the user experience.
